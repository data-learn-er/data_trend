Topics for discussion: An app for couples
BGM: 윤종신 - 이별하긴 하겠지 (Monthly Yoon JongShin - We broke up [but could we delete?])

cf) 총 사용자 수 = Total number of active users / 신규 기기 = Number of new install / 삭제 기기 - Number of uninstall

1) Usage trend for Couple App should be used in a different way compared to other apps. Will the market be formed through periodic departure and re-entry?
2) In Korea, there is a saying that spring and autumn are good periods to love. Do you think that there are many couples in autumn and winter rather than in spring and summer?
3) We should also consider the characteristics of users (Mostly in their 20s). Are they breaking up in the Midterm exam for the first semester and during their vacation? (*In Korea, the semester starts from every march)
4) What would be the most critical reason for the decline in the number of active users after Corona Outbreak? Would it be based on the number of break up? Or because couples don't have things to record (photos or events)?
